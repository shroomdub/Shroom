About

Shroom is a producer/DJ from Denver crafting a gritty, trippy strain of bass music that lives between breakbeat, deep dubstep, experimental bass, and downtempo. His tracks balance high-energy moments with heady, subtle touches, favoring rhythm-forward sound design, elastic percussion, and wet textures that come alive on big systems. Typical sets move through 90–110 and 140–150 BPM, inviting bodies to feel the low-end while giving the room permission to be themselves—fun, weird, and positive. Influences span Dmtree, Gimmie Gimmie, and Oddly Eazy, alongside a broader pull from nature and everyday atmosphere. Shroom’s focus is simple: build a community anchored in positivity, inclusivity, and mental-health advocacy, and deliver bass music that hits hard while leaving space to breathe.
